Bacon Q Dog

Bacon Q. Dog is a 9yr old labradoodle. He prefers to spend his days lounging among the three different beds/couches that his family has gifted him. He enjoys a walk or two around the neighborhood, as long as he can pretend that he doesn't see any of the other animals to avoid the embarrassment of not wanting to admit he has no wolf-like skills in chasing them.

At night just as the rest of the family is ready to relax, Bacon suddenly wants to release all of his energy. He will place his toys on a mini couch and frantically drag the couch around, giving his toys "a ride." There is also a lot of rolling. Lots and lots of rolling.

British Shorthair Cat

The British Shorthair cat is known for its distinctive appearance and charming personality. These cats are famous for their dense, plush coat, round face, and expressive eyes. They are often referred to as "teddy bears" due to their adorable, cuddly appearance.

Originating from Britain, these cats have a rich history and are considered one of the oldest cat breeds. They are known for their calm and easygoing nature, making them excellent companions for families and individuals alike.

Characteristics

  • Thick, plush coat in various colors and patterns
  • Rounded face with large, expressive eyes
  • Friendly and sociable temperament
  • Adaptable to indoor living
  • Low-maintenance grooming needs

Maine Coon Cat

The Maine Coon cat is a majestic and large breed known for its friendly personality and striking appearance. Originating from the state of Maine in the United States, these cats are often referred to as "gentle giants" due to their substantial size and gentle nature.

Maine Coons have a distinctive appearance with their tufted ears, bushy tails, and tufted paws. They are known for their luxurious, water-repellent fur, making them well-suited to cold climates. Their friendly and sociable disposition makes them excellent companions for families.

Characteristics

  • Large, muscular build with a bushy tail
  • Long, flowing fur in various colors and patterns
  • Friendly, sociable, and affectionate temperament
  • Intelligent and playful nature
  • Well-adapted to both indoor and outdoor living

Hamsters

Hamsters are small, adorable rodents that make popular pets for people of all ages. They are known for their tiny size, cute appearance, and playful behavior. Hamsters come in various species, with the Syrian hamster and Dwarf hamster being the most common choices as pets.

These furry creatures are easy to care for and are ideal for individuals living in small spaces. They are known for their ability to store food in their cheek pouches, which is both entertaining and functional in the wild.

Photo Gallery

Characteristics

  • Small size, typically 4-7 inches in length
  • Nocturnal creatures, active at night
  • Excellent climbers and diggers
  • Enjoy running on exercise wheels
  • Can live in cages or enclosures with bedding

Parrots

Parrots are a diverse group of birds known for their striking colors, intelligence, and remarkable vocal abilities. They belong to the family Psittacidae and are found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

Parrots come in various sizes and colors, with some of the most well-known species including the African Grey Parrot, Scarlet Macaw, and Cockatiel. These birds are highly social and thrive in environments with plenty of mental stimulation and interaction.

Photo Gallery

Characteristics

  • Vibrant plumage in a wide range of colors
  • Strong, hooked beak for eating and climbing
  • Highly intelligent and capable of learning tricks and mimicking sounds
  • Sociable and affectionate, forming strong bonds with their owners
  • Long lifespan, with some species living for several decades

Axolotls

Axolotls, also known as Mexican walking fish, are fascinating aquatic creatures native to Mexico. They belong to the salamander family and are known for their unique and captivating appearance. Unlike most amphibians, axolotls retain their larval features throughout their entire lives.

These small, aquatic beings are fully aquatic and never undergo metamorphosis to become terrestrial. They have external gills, frilly crests on their heads, and a charming, smiley appearance. Axolotls come in various colors, including shades of pink, white, and brown.

Photo Gallery

Characteristics

  • Permanently aquatic, with external gills for breathing underwater
  • Regenerative abilities, capable of regrowing lost body parts
  • Peaceful and gentle temperament, making them popular as pets
  • Native to Xochimilco and Chalco, two lakes in Mexico
  • Endangered species due to habitat loss and pollution
